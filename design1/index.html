About Us

Chelmsford's Finest Barbershops

With two premium locations in Chelmsford, Man About Town and New London Barbers have been serving the community with exceptional grooming services for years. Our commitment to traditional barbering techniques combined with contemporary styles ensures every client leaves looking and feeling their best.

Our experienced team of barbers pride themselves on attention to detail, personalized service, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where every visit is an experience. Whether you're after a classic cut, modern fade, or traditional hot towel shave, we've got you covered.

Step into either of our shops and discover why we're Chelmsford's most trusted barbershops.
