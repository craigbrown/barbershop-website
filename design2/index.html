Two Locations, One Standard

Experience premium barbering at either of our Chelmsford locations. Whether you prefer the established charm of Man About Town or the contemporary vibe of New London Barbers, you'll receive the same exceptional service and attention to detail.

Man About Town

Traditional Excellence

Our flagship location on New London Road has been serving the gentlemen of Chelmsford for years. Combining time-honored techniques with modern precision, we offer an authentic barbershop experience where craftsmanship meets conversation.

  • Classic hot towel shaves
  • Traditional scissor cuts
  • Beard sculpting & grooming
  • Comfortable, relaxed atmosphere
New London Barbers

Modern Styling

Our newer location brings a fresh, contemporary approach to men's grooming. Specializing in modern cuts, fades, and styling, we cater to clients who want to stay ahead of the trends while maintaining that classic barbershop quality and service.

  • Contemporary fades & tapers
  • Modern styling & texture work
  • Skin fades & detailed work
  • Contemporary, energetic vibe
